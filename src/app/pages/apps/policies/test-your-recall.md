# Privacy Policy for Test Your Recall

**Last updated:** March 6, 2026

Test Your Recall ("we," "our," or "us") is a mobile application available on iOS and Android. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our app.

---

## 1. Information We Collect

### 1.1 Account and Login Information

- **Email and password:** When you create an account with email and password, we collect and store your email address. Your password is securely hashed and not stored in plain text.
- **Username:** If you choose to create an account, we collect the username you provide. Usernames may be shown on leaderboards or in other in-app features.
- **Sign-in with Apple or Google:** If you use these options, we receive from the provider the information you have allowed them to share (such as an identifier, email, or name) in order to create and manage your account. We also store the username you choose. Each provider’s use of your data is governed by their own privacy policy.

### 1.2 Game and Profile Data (Logged-In Users)

When you are signed in, we collect and store:

- Game statistics (e.g., best score, games played, best time, daily streak)
- Daily challenge results (e.g., correct and total counts, score, time). We use this data to compute daily rankings and badges (e.g., winner, top 5%, top 10%).
- The date you last played (for features like streaks)

This data is used to provide gameplay features, leaderboards, and your in-app profile.

### 1.3 Friends and Versus Mode

If you use the friends or versus (head-to-head) features, we collect and store:

- **Friend relationships:** When you add a friend or accept a friend request, we store the association between you and that user. Other users can search for you by username to send friend requests.
- **Friend requests:** We store pending, accepted, and declined friend requests (sender and receiver identifiers and status).
- **Versus challenges:** When you create or participate in a versus challenge, we store the challenge (e.g., difficulty, random seed), who is playing, and each player’s result (score, time, correct/total counts). Your username and results are visible to your friend/opponent for that challenge.

Usernames are searchable by other logged-in users for the purpose of adding friends and starting versus games.

### 1.3.1 Reporting and Blocking Users

If you experience abusive or objectionable behaviour from another user, you can **report** them using the in-app Report option (available from the Friends list and the daily leaderboard). This opens an email to us with the reported user’s details so we can respond and take action where appropriate. You may also contact us directly at apps@orange-dev.com to report abuse; we will respond to reports in line with our terms.

You can **block** users from the Friends list or the daily leaderboard. Blocked users are hidden from your leaderboard and from friend search results. You can view and unblock users at any time in Settings → Blocked users. Block data is stored only on your device.

### 1.4 Guest Mode

You may use the app in guest mode without creating an account. In guest mode, we do not collect or store account information or game statistics on our servers. Some data may still be stored on your device (for example, to remember your guest session), and our advertising provider may collect data when ads are shown (see Section 2.2).

### 1.5 Information We Do Not Collect via Our Own Analytics

We do not use Google Analytics, Firebase, or other third-party analytics SDKs inside the app. The only analytics we use are the aggregate, non-personally-identifying reports provided by the Apple App Store and Google Play (e.g., downloads, platform, or region). We do not receive your name, email, or device ID from these store analytics.

## 2. Third-Party Services and Data Collection

### 2.1 Authentication and Backend (Supabase)

We use Supabase to handle:

- User sign-up, sign-in, and account management
- Storing your account details (e.g., email, username) and game statistics

Data you provide for your account and gameplay is processed and stored by Supabase in accordance with their privacy practices. For more information, see [Supabase Privacy Policy](https://supabase.com/privacy).

### 2.2 Advertising (Google AdMob)

The app shows ads (including interstitial and rewarded ads) provided by Google AdMob. When ads are shown, Google may collect information such as device identifiers, IP address, and ad interaction data for ad delivery, measurement, and fraud prevention. This is governed by Google’s policies, not ours.

If you purchase our ad-free option, you will not see ads in the app, and AdMob will not collect data from you for ad serving in our app.

For more information, see [Google Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy) and [Google’s ad-related disclosures](https://policies.google.com/technologies/ads).

### 2.3 In-App Purchases (Apple and Google)

In-app purchases (including the ad-free subscription) are processed by Apple (App Store) or Google (Play Store). We do not collect, see, or store your payment card or other payment details. To verify purchases and prevent fraud, we receive from the store and store on our servers transaction identifiers and receipt or token data (e.g., for subscription status and expiry). We use this only to enable the ad-free experience and to validate renewals. Purchase and payment data are handled according to Apple’s and Google’s respective privacy policies.

### 2.4 OAuth Providers (Apple, Google)

When you sign in with Apple or Google, your interaction with that provider is subject to their privacy policy. We only receive the information they make available to us (as described in Section 1.1) to create and manage your account.

### 2.5 Device Permissions (e.g. Audio, Notifications)

On Android, the app may request audio-related permissions (e.g. for in-app music playback). We use these only to play sounds and music within the app. We do not record your voice or any other audio from your device.

The app may ask for permission to show **notifications**. If you grant it, we use this only to schedule a **local reminder** on your device (e.g., “Complete the daily challenge!” at a time you choose, such as 9:00 AM). On Android, we may also request permission to schedule exact alarms so that reminder can fire at the chosen time. These reminders are created and stored on your device only; we do not use a push notification server, and we do not send your data to any third party for notifications. You can revoke notification permission or disable notifications for the app at any time in your device settings.

## 3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

- Create and manage your account and profile
- Provide gameplay, leaderboards, daily challenges (including daily rankings and badges), and related features
- Provide friends and versus (head-to-head) features, including friend search, friend requests, and challenge results visible to you and your opponent
- Display ads (when you have not purchased the ad-free option)
- Process and honor in-app purchases (e.g., ad-free), including storing transaction and receipt/token data for verification
- Send you optional local reminders (e.g., daily challenge reminder) if you enable notifications
- Send you service-related messages (e.g., password reset emails)
- Comply with legal obligations and protect our rights and safety

We do not sell your personal information.

## 4. Data Storage and Security

- **Our systems:** Account and game data are stored on infrastructure provided by Supabase. We rely on industry-standard security practices and Supabase’s safeguards.
- **Your device:** Some data is stored on your device (e.g., via AsyncStorage) for session persistence, guest mode, ad-free purchase status, and local preferences (such as whether you’ve completed the tutorial or started or completed the daily challenge in guest mode). This stays on your device unless you delete the app or clear its data.

We cannot guarantee absolute security of data transmitted over the internet or stored on any system, but we take reasonable steps to protect your information.

---

## 5. Data Retention

- **Account and game data:** We retain your account and game data for as long as your account exists. If you delete your account (see Section 7), we will delete or anonymize your personal data as described there.
- **Local device data:** Data stored only on your device (e.g., session, guest mode, ad-free flag) is removed when you uninstall the app or clear its data.

## 6. Children’s Privacy

The app is not directed at children under 13 (or under 16 in the EEA/UK). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children in those age groups. If you believe we have collected such information, please contact us and we will delete it.

Depending on where you live, you may have rights to:

- **Access** the personal data we hold about you
- **Correct** inaccurate data
- **Delete** your data and account
- **Object** to or **restrict** certain processing
- **Data portability** (e.g., a copy of your data in a usable format)

To exercise these rights, please contact us using the details in Section 10. You can delete your account at any time from within the app (profile menu → Settings → Delete account). We will respond to requests within a reasonable time and in line with applicable law.

- **Notifications:** You can disable notifications for the app at any time in your device settings, or decline the permission when the app asks. This will stop local reminders (e.g., daily challenge reminder).
- **Guest mode:** You can limit account-related collection by using guest mode, though ad-related collection may still apply when ads are shown.
